The Redeeming Sovereign Model

A protocol where debt burns, equity flows, and poverty ends.

Overview

Founded by Carlos J. Perez, The Redeeming Sovereign Model proposes a revolutionary sovereign digital currency designed to:

  • Automatically burn repaid debt
  • Embed savings and equity in every citizen's wallet
  • Incentivize essential goods, tax excessive luxury
  • End poverty by restructuring money's core logic

About the Founder

Carlos J. Perez is a seasoned entrepreneur, economist-in-training, and visionary system designer. With over 30 years of experience in the real economy and a deep commitment to social transformation, Carlos has created The Redeeming Sovereign Model as a blueprint for ending poverty through structural reform. Currently pursuing advanced studies in economics, he blends academic insight with real-world leadership to build systems that honor dignity, equity, and sustainability.
