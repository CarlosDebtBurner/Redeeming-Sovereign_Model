Meet Carlos J. Perez

Carlos J. Perez is a visionary entrepreneur and economist with over 30 years of experience in the service industry and business leadership. He is the creator of The Redeeming Sovereign Model, an economic framework designed to restore dignity, economic mobility, and community-based prosperity.

From founding restaurants in the Pacific Northwest to pursuing an Economics degree at the University of Washington, Carlos integrates real-world experience with academic rigor to challenge the current macroeconomic status quo.

Our Mission

To replace cycles of debt and inequality with a sovereign system that empowers people through infrastructure, education, and equity-focused development.
