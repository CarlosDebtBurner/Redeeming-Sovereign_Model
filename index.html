What is the Redeeming Sovereign Model?

The RSM is an alternative monetary model that challenges the traditional debt-centered system. It promotes economic systems built around:

  • Debt forgiveness and sustainable credit design
  • Local and sovereign monetary empowerment
  • Public investment in education, infrastructure, and people
  • Wealth circulation instead of concentration

Why It Matters

In a world where inequality is systemic and economic crises repeat, the RSM proposes a structural shift. Its goal: to replace austerity with dignity, and speculation with purpose.
